Maharashtrian Feast

This thali includes all the popular dishes in Marathi tradition, accompanied by a banana leaf for everyone to traditionally arrange the food. You are then set to experience authentic royal Maharashtrian cuisine. Our Pangat comes home delivered in a cute jute bag, in which we bring you more than 18+ different types of traditional Maharashtrian dishes sent along with banana leaves and a DIY decoration box to decorate your Pangat beautifully! You can order the Pangat as a traditional feast to savor casually with family and friends or on any special occasion like festivals, birthdays & anniversaries, pre-wedding celebrations, etc.
